Don't Forget Me When I Disappear

By: Deepesh
Jul 01, 2025 - 20:45

You were the light

that held my darkness still.

Even if your song was incomplete,

I longed to hear it,

just once,

from your trembling lips.

.

.

Your face,

your laughter—

they were fragile anchors.

And I,

a crumbling soul,

begged for you to steady me.

.

.

Your days—

they shine.

But my nights?

They ache,

missing the moon

that used to be you.

.

.

My arms lie empty,

but all my words—

they still carry your name.

.

.

I tried…

God knows I tried

to hold us together.

Then why do our hearts

now hide in corners

where even we can’t find them?

.

.

If you ever recall

those days fate gifted us,

please—don’t let them slip

like broken glass

falling into silence.

.

.

If our pictures fade,

or memories blur,

please—

don’t forget me.

.

.

Don’t forget the creases

on my sheets,

the places where we used to rest

between heartbreaks.

.

.

Don’t play with this heart

as if it’s a story

you’ve stopped reading.

.

.

Hold me once more,

if only in memory.

.

.

You were my moon.

My verses.

My silence.

And even now,

in this emptiness,

I’d write your name

on blank pages

just to remember

what it felt like

to be loved.

.

.

We got

on these roads

that once led to each other.

.

.

But if you can—

just once—

remember my smile.

Remember what we were.

.

.

Don’t forget me

if I disappear.

.

.

Please...

don’t forget me.
